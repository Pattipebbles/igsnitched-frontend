About IGsnitched

Welcome to IGsnitched – your ultimate ally in the pursuit of clarity and accountability on Instagram. We know how it feels to wonder who’s lurking in the shadows, dropping sneaky follows and unfollows, or changing their bio like there’s no tomorrow. That’s why we built IGsnitched: to give you the receipts you deserve, without the headache of logging in or connecting your own account.

Our Mission

IGsnitched was created to empower individuals with insights into public Instagram activity. Whether you’re keeping tabs on a partner, watching over a brand, or simply satisfying your curiosity, our tools provide clear, concise reports delivered with a sprinkle of sass. We believe transparency shouldn’t be a luxury – it should be a right.

Why “Snitch”?

“Snitch” might sound cheeky, but we embrace it with pride. Our service pulls back the curtain on IG behavior that would otherwise remain hidden. We don’t judge – we just deliver the facts (and maybe a little side-eye from Brad Mode).
